A Ninja & His Aakriti

Scroll slowly... every word is real 🌙

In a land of eternal cherry blossoms and moonlit rivers, there lived a Ninja — feared by the world, unknown to himself. He wore silence like armour... but inside, he was searching for something he couldn't name. 🥷

And then came Aakriti — the most adorable, unexpected, wonderful little piggy in all the realm 🐷💕 She didn't arrive with fanfare or magic spells. She simply appeared... and everything changed.

Because of her, the Ninja started trying. Pushing. Growing. Not because he had to — but because he wanted to become someone worthy of her. Someone her world could trust. Someone her family could respect. Someone she'd be proud of.

She healed parts of him he never spoke about. Her love, her support, her understanding — it was medicine for the soul. He'd sit quietly sometimes, just thinking — how did someone like her become mine? 🌸

The dark forces of doubt, distance and time tried to stand between them. But they forgot one thing — this Ninja doesn't quit. He'll leave his comfort zone, work through every night, cross every mountain — not for glory, but for her.

And so the story isn't finished. It's still being written — one effort, one day, one "I love you" at a time. 💗
